About us

Pukulan Pentjak Silat Serak is an Indonesian martial art developed by Pak Serak, who was severely disabled, yet one of the most esteemed martial artists of Indonesia in the 18th Century. We have received the art from the Dutch Indonesian family of the De Vries and De Thouars. Our lineage is from Pendekar Victor I.C. De Thouars.

Serak International Asia is the SE Asia regional body of Serak International and the VDT Academy under the leadership and supervision of Maha Guru Horacio Rodriguez.
